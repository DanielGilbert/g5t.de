7D Mad Rogue Kart

7D Mad Rogue Kart is a rogue-like, turn-based kart racing game, build in one week during the 7DRL 2023. Your goal is to finish first, while racing against characters you might be familiar with.

Santa Paravia en Fiumaccio

Santa Paravia en Fiumaccio is a text-based resource management game set in Italy in the 15th century. Originally written by George Blank in 1978 for the TRS-80, translated to C in 2003 by Thomas Knox and finally translated to C# in 2025 by yours truly. This article does a deep dive into the game.
